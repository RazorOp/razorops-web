Frequently Asked Questions

What is the procedure to join the Razorops Partners Program?
All you need to do is follow a few simple steps. First, book a meeting with the Razorops team or write to us at support@razorops.com. Then discuss the program in detail along with the benefits and join.
Who can join the Razorops Partners program to earn commission?
Any company can join — startups, small businesses, and full-fledged organisations — and earn commissions.
How much can partners earn as commission?
Commissions for the Partners Program are 10% of the subscription plan selected by the subscriber.
What are the prime benefits offered to partners?
  • High visibility through contributed content and co-marketing initiatives
  • Pre-sales support
  • Chance to earn commissions
What is the difference between the Referral Program & Partners Program?
The Razorops Referral Program is for individuals to earn commissions, whereas the Partners Program is for companies to join and earn commissions.
