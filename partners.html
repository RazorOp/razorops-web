Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What happens if we don’t meet the annual revenue requirement?

We give each partner a 6 month grace period. If this commitment still hasn’t been met, partners may be required to move to a lower tier level.

Q. What does the onboarding process entail?

After a partner agreement is signed, we will issue you a welcome packet and a partner ID number. We will then set up an onboarding call to make sure you have everything you need to start earning commission.

Q. Can I register a lead to be exclusive to us as a partner?

Yes, we have a deal registration process which allows you to register a lead to be aligned to you as a partner. In order for a lead to qualify for deal registration, the end user company must have shown a genuine interest in Civo or attended a partner qualifying event.

Q. Do you have other non-commission based partner programs?

Yes! If our partner program above doesn’t seem like the best fit for you, we are happy to chat about other options which may better suit your requirements. Why not set up a meeting today?
