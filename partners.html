Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the procedure to join the Razorops Partners Program?

All you need to do is follow a few simple steps. First step to join the Razorops Partners Program is to book a meeting with the Razorops team or you can write to us at support@razorops.com. Thereafter discuss the program in detail along with the benefits and join the program.

Q. Who can join the Razorops Partners program to earn commission?

Any company can join the Razorops Partners program- startups, small businesses, full fledged organisations, and earn commissions.

Q. How much amount can partners earn as commission?

The commissions for the Partners Program will be 10% of the subscription plan selected by the subscriber.

Q. What are the prime benefits offered to the partners?

The prime benefits offered to the partners through Razorops Partners Program are-

  • High Visibility through contributed content and co-marketing initiatives.
  • Pre- Sales Support
  • Chance to earn commissions

Q. What is the difference between Razorops Referral Program & Razorops Partners Program?

As the title suggests Razorops Referral Program is for individuals to earn commissions whereas the Razorops Partners Program is for companies to join and earn commissions.
