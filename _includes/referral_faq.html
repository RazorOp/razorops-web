Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I refer a friend?

Your referral program area will provide you a unique referral link. Share this link with your friends and family.

Q. Will I receive my referral bonus immediately?

Referred users must be active more than 30 days to be counted toward your referral bonus .

Q. When are payouts issued?

Payouts are issued on the business day following the 1st and 15th of each month.
