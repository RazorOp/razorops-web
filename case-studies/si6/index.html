Si6 is free to focus on new features with Razorops

How Razorops Helped Si6

Si6 is an Enterprise Platform for Managing Education. It is building an Enterprise Platform for Institutions to adapt to the changing business models and to manage every student's life cycle. It is used to manage institute networks, Academic structure, faculty, resources and fee structures.

The Challenge

To keep step up with gestures of feature and service updates, Si6 wanted to have a CI/CD pipeline without the need to manage the infrastructure. Si6, which is an online education platform, the development downtime and lost momentum leads to slow down in its productivity, and also while deploying the changes.

Why Razorops ?

RAZOROPS is a Saas based CI/CD platform .It is one of best CI/CD tools because it supports, runs tests, staging, all within pipelines. RAZOROPS is easy to maintain and easy to use. With Razorops, there will be very little overhead and it helps to focus on what matters.

Solution

We chose Razorops because we believe in the power of SaaS. Razorops was effective, easy to integrate and easy to configure. With Razorops, Si6 team was able to have a stable and authentic partner in terms of quality assurance and integration flows. Productivity for our team became much easier because we were able to extract and outsource yet another part of our responsibilities.

"Razorops takes care of configuration and assets in the background, and that will liberate a lot of my time."

Srikanth B.

Founder and Director - Si6 Associates
