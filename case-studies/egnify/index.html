Moving fast, making customers happy with Razorops

How Razorops Helped Egnify

Egnify is an Edu-tech company that aims to make education simpler and accessible to all. Egnify provides an online platform to learners and educators accessible from anywhere and everywhere.

The Challenge

With the growing number of users, they were facing huge traffic on our servers during weekends leading it to crash at times. It was affecting the quality of their service. We were hoping to resolve this issue as quickly as possible. We were looking for a solution that could-

  • Automate the server's capacity as per the traffic
  • Increase the speed making it faster

Why Razorops ?

Razorops is a container native continuous delivery platform to automate your workflow from development to production in a few simple steps.

Solution

Razorops team helped us in scaling DevOps across our systems. Using Razorops, a container-native continuous integration and delivery platform used to automate testing and deployment across the environments, we were able to automate the server, making it more efficient in scaling up or down depending on the user traffic.

"We are very happy with Razorops, we can now focus on our goal without worrying about trivial matters. Choosing Razorops was the right choice."

Kiran Babu

Founder and CEO of Egnify
