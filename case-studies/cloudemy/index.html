Applozic uses Razorops to focus on fast delivery without any compromises

About Applozic

Applozic is a Real-Time Chat and Messaging API for Developers which makes communication easy from both the web or mobile with push notification, video and voice, and cross-platform integration.

The Challenge

Applozic, being a Real-Time Chat and Messaging platform, needed faster iterations and quicker deliveries to end customers.

They needed a DevOps solution for :

  • Ensure faster deployments
  • Stabilize the work environments
  • Significant improvement in product quality
  • Continuous delivery of software

The Solution- How Razorops Helped Applozic?

The ultimate goal was to have an automated testing and deployment pipeline that would allow the team to focus on development rather than worrying about deploying the bits. By shifting over to Razorops, Applozic has seen a significant increase in its ability to ship frequently.

"A quick and authentic CI/CD solution is critical. It verifies features get to our customers on the dot and bug-free."

Satya Krishna Ganni

CEO of Applozic
